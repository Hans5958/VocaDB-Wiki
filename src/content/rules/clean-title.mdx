The primary names and the aliases (if any) of a song entry should only contain the name of songs. Any other details should not be included, such as, but not limited to:

- Vocalists, including voice synths (e.g. "feat. Hatsune Miku")
- Song types (e.g. "Official Music Video", "Vocaloid Original", "Cover")
- Producers, or any other artists
- Voice data availability (e.g. "+UST", "+VSQ")

Exception exists for remix titles, see [the "Keep remix titles" rule](https://wiki.vocadb.net/rules/keep-remix-titles) for details.

> **Note**
>
> In most derivatives/alternate versions, you may only need to copy the name of the original version. You can open the original version as reference when adding/editing an entry.

## Examples

The following examples are extracted from (English) video titles, as if you don't edit the name that is automatically inserted when creating a song entry.

For "[Alien Alien](https://vocadb.net/S/106773)".

> **Incorrect**
>
> <s>NayutalieN</s> - Alien Alien <s>(ft. Hatsune Miku) [Official Music Video]</s>

- "NayutalieN" is the producer (added as an artist)
- "Hatsune Miku" is the vocalist (added as an artist)
- "Official Music Video" indicates the song type (covered in the song entry type)

> **Correct**
>
> Alien Alien

For "[CYBERPUNK DEAD BOY](https://vocadb.net/S/582638)":

> **Incorrect**
>
> CYBERPUNK DEAD BOY <s>feat. HATSUNE MIKU / Maiki P</s>

- "Maiki P" is the producer (added as an artist)
- "HATSUNE MIKU" is the vocalist (added as an artist)

> **Correct**
>
> CYBERPUNK DEAD BOY

For [a cover of "Bite Me"](https://vocadb.net/S/378730):

> **Incorrect**
>
> <s>【SOLARIA】</s>Bite Me <s>(Avril Lavigne)【English SynthV Cover】+SVP</s> 

- "SOLARIA" is the vocalist (added as an artist)
- "Avril Lavigne" is the vocalist of the original song (added as an artist on [the cover unifier, if any](https://vocadb.net/T/6751))
- "English SynthV Cover" indicates the song type (covered in the song entry type)
- "+SVP" indicates the song data availabiltiy (added as [a tag](https://vocadb.net/T/7225))

> **Correct**
>
> Bite Me
