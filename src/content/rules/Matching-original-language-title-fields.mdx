The "Original language" title field should be filled in the corresponding "Primary name" field; the former should not be Unspecified, and the latter should not be empty (unless tagged with [untitled](https://vocadb.net/T/6335/untitled)).

## Examples

For "[BUTCHER VANITY](https://vocadb.net/S/605339)", an English song, the "Original language" field is set to English, while its English "Primary name" field contains its proper title and is thus not empty.

![Example with BUTCHER VANITY. The left side is incorrect because "Non-English" is selected as the "Original language".](public/images/rules/matching-original-language-title-fields/ex-en.png)

For "[PITUMPUTPITONGPUTINGTUPA](https://vocadb.net/S/527071)", a Filipino song, the same applies but with the selection of "Non-English".

![Example with PITUMPUTPITONGPUTINGTUPA. The left side is incorrect because "English" is selected as the "Original language".](public/images/rules/matching-original-language-title-fields/ex-ph.png)
