If a voice synth song also has an official human-sung version and both of them are published less than **a week** apart, the (voice synth) song entry should have [the "Original song" type](/rules/original-song-classification) and be tagged with [simultaneous upload](https://vocadb.net/T/4946).

Otherwise, if the human-sung version is published more than a week before the voice synth version, the song entry is a [cover](/rules/original-song-classification), tagged with [the "self-cover" tag](https://vocadb.net/T/391).

Add the human-sung version as the reference following [the "Specify original" rule](/rules/specify-original), plus the **publish date** (unless it exists on VocaDB/TouhouDB/UtaiteDB).

These rules do not apply if both songs have entries on VocaDB.
